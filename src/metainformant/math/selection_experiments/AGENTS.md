## Module Components

### Selection Models
- **Directional Selection**: Models for artificial and natural directional selection
- **Stabilizing Selection**: Fitness functions favoring intermediate phenotypes
- **Disruptive Selection**: Models promoting phenotypic diversification
- **Frequency-dependent Selection**: Fitness depending on genotype frequencies

- **Breeding Value Estimation**: Prediction of genetic merit
- **Heritability Calculation**: Narrow and broad sense heritability estimation

- **Individual Selection**: Fitness effects at the individual level
- **Group Selection**: Selection acting on group-level traits
- **Kin Selection**: Inclusive fitness and Hamilton's rule implementation
- **Major Transitions**: Models for evolutionary transitions in individuality
